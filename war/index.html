These are the rules:

  • Deal: starts game and deals cards
  • Bet: Player determines how much they want to bet(10, 20, 50, 100)
  • If Player and Dealer are dealt matching cards Player will be asked would they want to play war or surrender.
  • War: If Player says war when prompted cards will be dealt again for possibly winning double the bet OR losing double the bet.
  • Surrender: If Player says surrender when prompted the game will end and Player loses bet.
